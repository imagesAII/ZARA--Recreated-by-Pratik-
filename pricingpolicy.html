PRICING POLICY

Zara may revise item prices during a campaign period.

Items carry the same price in the online shop and in physical stores. When website and label prices differ, the amount shown at checkout is taken as the valid price.

Price changes are not applied retroactively to products whose initial prices have already been reduced.
