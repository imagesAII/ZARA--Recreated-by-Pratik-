Payment Methods

UPI Payment

Unified Payments Interface (UPI) allows instant bank-to-bank payments using mobile apps.

Supported Apps: Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM

Example:

  • UPI ID: rahul.kumar@upi
  • Amount: ₹2,499
  • Transaction Status: Successful

Debit / Credit Card

Card payments are processed securely using encrypted payment gateways.

Accepted Cards: Visa, MasterCard, RuPay

Example:

  • Card Type: Visa Credit Card
  • Card Number: **** **** **** 4578
  • Transaction Amount: ₹4,999
  • Payment Status: Approved

Cash on Delivery (COD)

Cash on Delivery allows customers to pay in cash at the time of product delivery.

Example:

  • Order Amount: ₹1,799
  • Payment Mode: Cash
  • Payment Collected By: Delivery Executive
  • Payment Status: Pending until delivery

COD is available only for eligible locations and orders.
